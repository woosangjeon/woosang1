Wir, das Möbiusband

2021

A dissonance of the self.

Through a series of looping actions and shifting spatial compositions, two performers embody the paradox of a self unmoored from its core identity. The work visualizes emotional disconnection and internal conflict, drawing the audience into a cycle of repetition and distortion. In a fleeting rupture, a glimpse of the true self emerges, only to be reabsorbed into the false world.

  • Co-Director Jong Ha Kim & Woosang Jeon
