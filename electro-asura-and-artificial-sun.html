Electro Asura and Artificial Sun

2024

"Electro Asura and Artificial Sun" is Aisthesis Savage's latest experimental work, a collaboration with Korean award-winning dancer Woosang and Korean art production group Channel Crane. This cross-disciplinary new media theater performance integrates artificial intelligence, enhanced electro-tactile technology, and immersive experiences.

Concept and Technology

The work demonstrates the path of human transformation into a cybernetic Asura, where dancers interface with Aisthesis Savage's mechanical prosthetics and augmented sensory technology, engaging in dialogue with the embedded LLM-driven AI Agent Sun to explore human perceptual mutations in the cyber age.

Performance Experience

The piece presents an unprecedented immersive performance combining large language models, immersive theater, and mechanical prosthetics. Through data sculpture, artificial intelligence, and generative immersive experiences, we challenge audience perceptions of performance art at C-Lab's Dome in Taipei.
