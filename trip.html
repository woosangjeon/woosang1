trip

2020

The year is 2020, and the world is gripped by a virus that has become endemic, subtly altering the fabric of daily life. As I walk through this altered landscape, I encounter fleeting glimpses of lives unfolding, some tethered to the digital realm, others grounded in the tangible world. The lines between online and offline are blurred, the familiar becomes uncanny, the ordinary becomes surreal. In this space where reality is distorted and reshaped, the grotesque and the mundane coexist in a delicate balance that influences our perception. This work invites the viewer to traverse the liminal spaces between confinement and freedom, reality and illusion, prompting a reflection on the human condition in unprecedented times.
